Frequently Asked Questions

Are these books really free?

Titles marked Free are in the public domain or shared by the publisher at no cost. Paid titles link to official stores.

How do I read eBooks?

Download the EPUB/PDF to your reader app or open in‑browser. Audiobooks play in any modern browser or podcast app.

Can I request a book?

Yes—use the contact form and we’ll try to add it to the library or suggest similar reads.
